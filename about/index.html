Our History

P S.jpg P R.jpg

Two friends of over 25 years formed FABtekk Engineering in 2014, they joined skill sets to do what they both are hugely passionate about; Engineering.

Paul Stephenson has over 30 years experience in fabrication and product development in manufacturing Nationwide. Paul Read an engineer equals in experience in the food industry and has led installation and refurbishment projects all across Europe.

Together they have worked hard to understand their clients and develop products and services that help make the lives of those in production that little bit easier. They have varied experiences and knowledge, which they use in tandem with the contacts they have acquired over the years to find a solution to every problem.

Having worked in the industry ‘hands on’, Paul and Paul, more than most are acutely aware of the fast paced and pressurised nature of running operations, for this reason the spirit of FABtekk is:

‘right first time and every time '

7 years on, FABtekk Engineering is widely respected as a quality service provider - The Directors intend to build on this success, continuing to deliver service and innovation for all existing and new customers.
